Refund Policy for Slots - Appointment Booking Application

Conditions for Full Refund:

  • Cancellation 24 Hours Prior: If a user cancels their appointment at least 24 hours before the scheduled time, they are eligible for a full refund of the appointment fee.

Refund Process:

  • Cancellation Timeframe: To qualify for a refund, cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled appointment time.
  • Refund Amount: Upon meeting the cancellation criteria, the full appointment fee will be refunded to the original payment method used during the booking process.

Exceptions:

  • Late Cancellations or No Shows: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled appointment time or no shows will not be eligible for a refund. The appointment fee will be non-refundable in such cases.

How to Request a Refund:

Users can request a refund by contacting our customer support team through the Slots application or by using the provided contact information.

Processing Time:

Refunds will be processed within a reasonable timeframe after the cancellation request is approved and confirmed.
